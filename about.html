About me

My Mission

Hey there! I’m a photographer who’s been obsessed with capturing light and life since I was 14 — which is basically forever ago in camera years.

Based in Antibes but always ready to pack up and chase the perfect shot across Cannes, Nice, Monaco.

I shoot concerts, portraits, weddings, and sometimes things you wouldn’t expect — like an archaeological documentary (yes, that’s a real thing I did).I like to think of myself as a photography swiss knife: adaptable, curious, and definitely not afraid to get my hands dirty to get the shot just right.

Fluent in English, French, Arabic, and some Italian, I’m here to turn your moments into stories — with a little bit of magic and a lot of heart.

New to the area, I’m offering special rates to build my local tribe. Let’s make something unexpected and unforgettable.

Lucie Orer Payen
